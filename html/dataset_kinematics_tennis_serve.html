Tennis serve kinematics#

This dataset was kindly provided by Fraje Watson, Steve Taylor and Jin (Derrick) Gaokuang from the University College London, Aspire CREATe lab.

These are the bilateral kinematics of four tennis serves, sampled at 50 Hz. The laboratory vertical axis is z.
